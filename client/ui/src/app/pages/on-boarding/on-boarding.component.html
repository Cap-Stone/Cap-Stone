Instructions

1. How to use map feature

1.Navigate to Map on Menu Bar

2. Zoom in and out:
If you use a computer, move your mouse to the area you want to zoom.Then rotate the mouse wheel to zoom in or zoom out to get the view you wish for.If you use a smart phone, use your two finger on the phonescreen to zoom in and out.


3.Put a pin on the map:
Click to the location you want to put a pin. A pop-up message will appear on the screen to confirm that you want to put the pin right there. Once you click confirm, a pin will be saved on the map.
For example, I want to put a pin between Ayers st and Gollihar rd.

Click on that location the press "Confirm"

Pin will be saved and displayed on the map view



2. How to display statistic

1.Navigate to Statistic on Menu Bar
2.Choose the month on the dropdownlist to see potetial clients statistic. Once you click on the month option, statistic graph will be displayed.

3.On the top-right conner of the statistic graph, we offer a print option. Simply click on it and choose the option you want.
